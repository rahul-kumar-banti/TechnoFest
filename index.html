Events

Two Days I.T.S-National Convention, an Inter-Institutional Techno-Cultural Fest being organized by the Institute of Technology & Science, Ghaziabad during 14th -15th March 2019. The event is aimed to provide an opportunity to the participating students from various institutes to come and flaunt their artistic, technical, literature and innovative skills.

This event comprises of four categories of events I.T.S National Convention is continual in series of well-known I.T.S Inter Institutional fests SAMAGRA & WYSWYG and include a series of Technical, Fine Arts, Literary and Cultural for participants to inculcate the spirit of competition and group dynamism among them.

General Guidelines
There is no participation fee
The content is open for all Graduate and Post Graduate students of all streams
Students must carry their valid College identity card or authentication letter issued by the institute
A team can participate in any number of events subject to the events are not in parallel
Rules for all the events are available on the website and decision of the judges will be final & binding

About us

About I.T.S The Education Group
I.T.S - The Education Group is an 23 years old leading educational group of the country. Its various programmes are NBA accredited & NAAC (A-Grade) accredited. ISO 9001:2008 certified group offers 20 courses to its 8000 students in its 8 Institutes spread over 4 campuses, endowed with state-of-the-art infrastructure, all modern facilities and more than 700 distinguished faculty members.
About I.T.S:
Institute of Technology and Science was established in the year 1995 with a view to impart quality education in the field of Management and Information Technology. Institute offers professional education through well accredited courses. It offers Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM, 2 Yrs, NBA accredited), Masters of Business Administration (MBA, 2Yrs), Masters of Computer Application (MCA, 3 Yrs, NBA accredited) and Masters of Computer Application (MCA, 2 Yrs, NBA accredited). Institute of Technology and Science boast of unparallel infrastructure which is evident from Institute's consistent high ranking on infrastructure parameter. Intellectual Capital at the Institute lies in eminent and experienced faculty members. The Institute is committed to provide practice oriented learning and a contemporary industry-focused curriculum, driven by strong industry interface. We foster all round development and enhancement of knowledge base in our students through various extra-curricular, co-curricular and curricular activities. Our bright young students have proved themselves and have embarked upon promising careers in the corporate world. Institute of Technology and Science is consistently working towards Creating Thinking Professional Order and contributing towards society at large with imparting valuable knowledge.
